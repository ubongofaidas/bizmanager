# BizManager Account Deletion Instructions

## How to delete your BizManager account and data

You can delete your account directly within the BizManager app:

### Steps to delete your account:

1. Open the BizManager app
2. Log into your account
3. Navigate to **More** tab (bottom navigation)
4. Tap on **Delete Account**
5. Confirm your password
6. Tap **Confirm Delete**

### What happens when you delete your account:

| Data Type | What happens |
|-----------|--------------|
| Personal information (name, email) | **Permanently deleted immediately** |
| Business transaction data | **Permanently deleted immediately** |
| Inventory records | **Permanently deleted immediately** |
| Customer data | **Permanently deleted immediately** |
| Sales reports | **Permanently deleted immediately** |
| Backup files (if any) | **Deleted within 7 days** |

### Data retention period:

- Account data: Deleted immediately upon confirmation
- Backup copies: Automatically removed within 7 days
- Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for 30 days (no longer linked to you)

### Need help?

If you cannot delete your account through the app, contact us at:
- **Email:** support@bizmanager.co.tz
- **Response time:** Within 48 hours

**Important:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Export any important data before deletion.
