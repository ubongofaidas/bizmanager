# BizManager Refund & Billing Policy

**Subscription Management & Financial Terms**

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 7, 2026

---

## 1. Subscription Plans & Billing

**Monthly Plans:** Billed in advance on the same date each month. No long-term commitment required.

**Annual Plans:** Billed annually.

All prices are in **Tanzanian Shillings (TZS)** unless otherwise specified.

## 2. Free Trial Period

New businesses receive a **14-day free trial** with full access to all plan features. No payment information required during trial. At trial end, you must select a paid plan to continue service. Trial data is retained for 30 days after trial expiration.

## 3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be requested within **7 days** of initial subscription payment if:

- (a) The service has experienced documented downtime exceeding 48 continuous hours
- (b) Critical features advertised are completely non-functional
- (c) You experience technical issues preventing basic POS operations that our support team cannot resolve within 48 hours

**Partial refunds** are calculated on a pro-rata basis for unused service days.

## 4. Non-Refundable Circumstances

No refunds will be issued for:

- (a) Change of business requirements or preferences
- (b) Insufficient usage of the platform
- (c) Failure to properly configure or use available features
- (d) Business closure or sale
- (e) Temporary service interruptions under 24 hours
- (f) Annual plan cancellations after 30 days of subscription

## 5. Cancellation Process

**To cancel your current subscription:** Navigate to More → Choose new plans → Cancel current subscription

**To delete your account:** Navigate to More → Delete Account (this will delete your entire account including all data)

## 6. Failed Payments & Account Suspension

Failed payments result in:

- **Day 1-3:** Payment retry attempts
- **Day 4-7:** Account downgraded to read-only mode
- **Day 8-14:** Service suspended, data preserved
- **Day 15+:** Account scheduled for deletion

Reactivation requires payment of outstanding balance plus 10% reinstatement fee.

## 7. Payment Methods & Processing

We accept:
- (a) Mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money)
- (b) Bank transfers (CRDB, NMB, NBC)
- (c) Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MasterCard)

All transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways as instructed in the app.

## 8. Plan Changes & Upgrades/Downgrades

- **Plan upgrades** take effect immediately with pro-rated charges
- **Downgrades** take effect at next billing cycle
- **Enterprise plan changes** require account manager consultation
- **Custom feature requests** may incur development charges

## 9. Dispute Resolution

Billing disputes must be submitted in writing to **billing@bizmanager.co.tz** within 14 days of charge. Include:
- (a) Transaction ID
- (b) Business name
- (c) Detailed dispute explanation

We will respond within 5 business days. Unresolved disputes may be referred to the Fair Competition Commission of Tanzania.
